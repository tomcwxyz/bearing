# Bearing

Bearing helps you find the right AI model for your task. Describe what you want to do, tell us what matters to you, and get a ranked shortlist with transparent scoring you can understand and trust.

Whether you're choosing a model for the first time or wondering if your current one is the best fit, Bearing gives you clear, honest recommendations across 29 models from 12 providers — scored on quality, cost, speed, privacy, sustainability, transparency, and capability.

## What can you do with Bearing?

- **Get a recommendation** — Describe your task, rank your priorities, and see which models are the best fit
- **Validate your current model** — Find out if you're overpaying or missing a better option
- **Compare two models head-to-head** — Same prompt, two models, real outputs side by side. You decide which is better.
- **Browse the model registry** — Explore 29 models with full capability, pricing, transparency, and sustainability data

## The open dataset

Every recommendation, selection, and preference vote is collected as anonymised data and published openly. No raw text, no prompts, no personal information — just task types, priorities, model choices, and outcomes.

This dataset is available to anyone building routing, recommendation, or evaluation tools. Download it from the [Data page](/data).
